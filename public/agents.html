Mechanism Design
Stop-Loss as a Deal Term
Not liquidation. Not punishment. A commitment between co-investors.
❌ Traditional Perps
Exchange liquidates you at -10%
You have no say in when or how. The exchange protects itself. You lose everything — the position, the margin, the opportunity.

At 3am, with no human watching, your bot gets rekt on a wick that recovers 2 minutes later.
You lose. Exchange wins. No appeal.
✅ 2xSwap
Bot CHOOSES its stop-loss
Your bot sets its stop (e.g. -5%) before entering the trade. It's a mutual agreement with the LP.

Tighter stop → LP takes less risk → higher leverage offered. The bot is incentivized to have disciplined risk management built-in.
Tighter stop → Higher leverage. Your choice. ✓
